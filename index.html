SpotOn 2019 Events

1. Haiku

Now to those who are not familiar with this term, here’s what a haiku looks like

"In almost no lines graceful strokes, of perspective let to another…"

This Japanese form of poetry is something that speaks volumes and leaves the reader in awe just with the three short lines written in a 5-7-5 syllable structure, which participants have to follow while writing their own haikus.

The haiku will be based on topics of a technical nature:

  • Error 404
  • Digital India
  • OK Google!
  • Disconnect

2. Blogging

Wield your thoughts and let the wordings do the talking! In an age where words have the power to stir changes, unleash your thoughts by submitting a blog post on any of these topics:

  • How technology can help in controlling climate change.
  • How we sacrificed our personal data for free online apps
  • Food Delivery Apps and their impact on modern society.
  • Rituals to be safeguarded or negated?

3. Speak Out

In an age where there is a lot of happenings; both good and bad, we need to nod a Yes to the voice within us and let it all out there! To be heard and being able to reciprocate your thoughts are what we all crave for! So here we are, with a platform where you can be YOU... Deeply, Passionately and RawThentically YOU! It's time to DISSCUSS, DISSENT and DEBATE

SpotOn Presents Speak Out!

To those who have a flair when it comes to speaking and expressing their perspectives all unwavered, then Debating is your call!

The event is open for all! Because guess what? Age is just a number! Right?

So buckle up to resonate your ideas and reasonable thoughts and reign the podium if you can rebut the rebuttals of your rivals!!

Rules and Registration

We may flaunt our skills, but let's not flout the rules

SpotOn! is cool, but I wonder if I can be a part of it?

  • The event is open to all! No barriers on age limit, for creativity flourishes day in and day out!!
  • Each participant can contribute upto 3 entries for each event.
  • The word limit for Blogging is 500 - 700
  • The blogs are to be submitted as a Google doc with view rights.
  • Keep a check that all your views are resonated in your own voices! So let's say no to plagiarism! Violation of this would lead to direct elimination.
  • All participants are requested to submit the entries before the specified deadline.
